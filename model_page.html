Some Notes on Use

Lasers can be moved by clicking and dragged. The can be angled by first selecting the desired laser, and then dragging the sphere in front of it.
To remove a laser, first click "Delete" then click the desired object to be removed.

Note that when switched to "mirror" only the first light-mirror interaction is accurate, but this is sufficient to show the focal point.

Model will reset on page refresh.

Camera movement is enabled under "camera controls"
